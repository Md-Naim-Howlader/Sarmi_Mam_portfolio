About Me

About Me

A dedicated entrepreneur with a clear vision — to deliver high-quality products while building brands people trust.

I am the Founder of Shopping Zone BD and Asmi Supershop. From the very beginning, I committed to excellence — ensuring every product and service reflects quality, consistency, and customer satisfaction.

Through Shopping Zone BD, I bring modern, stylish, and affordable fashion closer to people, while Asmi Supershop focuses on fulfilling everyday needs with reliability and convenience.

With a forward-thinking mindset, I continuously explore new opportunities to expand my businesses, introduce fresh ideas, and enhance the customer experience — all while making a positive impact through ethical business practices.
